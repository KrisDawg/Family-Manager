# Privacy Policy for Family Household Manager

**Effective Date:** January 16, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Family Household Manager ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Inventory Data**: Item names, categories, quantities, expiration dates, and storage locations
- **Shopping Lists**: Items you add to your shopping lists
- **Meal Plans**: Meal planning data and recipes

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, and app version
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the app (features used, time spent)

### 2.3 Permissions Requested
- **Camera**: Used for OCR (Optical Character Recognition) to scan receipts and inventory labels
- **Storage**: To save your database locally on your device
- **Internet**: Optional for future cloud sync features (currently not implemented)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide and maintain the app's core functionality
- Process OCR requests for receipt scanning
- Store your inventory and shopping data locally
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Provide customer support

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Storage
All your data is stored locally on your device using SQLite database. We do not transmit or store your data on our servers.

### 4.2 Data Security
- Your data never leaves your device
- Database is encrypted using SQLite's built-in security features
- No internet connection required for core functionality

### 4.3 Data Retention
Your data remains on your device until you uninstall the app or delete it manually.

## 5. Third-Party Services

### 5.1 OCR Processing
When you use the camera OCR feature:
- Images are processed locally on your device
- No images are sent to external servers
- OCR text extraction happens offline

### 5.2 Future Features
If we add cloud sync features in the future, we will update this policy and obtain your explicit consent.

## 6. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date."

## 8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
- Email: privacy@familyhouseholdmanager.com

## 9. Consent

By using Family Household Manager, you consent to this Privacy Policy.

## 10. Data Deletion

To delete all your data:
1. Open the app
2. Go to device settings
3. Clear app data or uninstall the app

Your data will be permanently deleted from your device.
